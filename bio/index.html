Elijah Odeny

Biography

Who is Elijah Odeny?

Elijah Odeny is a teacher by profession and he is the current Dean of Studies at Migowa Secondary in Siaya County

Early Life

He was born in 1974 at Bondo town in Siaya County

He attended Ndigwa Primary School and Chianda High School

He proceeded to the University of Nairobi for his University Education

Career

He trained as a teacher at the University of Nairobi and he is the current Dean of Studies at Migowa Secondary where he has dedicated his services for a very long time

Personal Life

He is a married man with kids and he is a resopnsible family man who take care of his family

Social Life

He loves watching movies and football

He is a netball coach and Arsenal fan
